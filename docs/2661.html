📈 Illinois Legalizes Blockchain Contracts

According to The state’s “Blockchain Technology Act,” Illinois became the latest state to recognize smart contracts and other blockchain-based records as legal instruments. A smart contract, a record or signature may not be denied legal effect or enforceability solely because a blockchain was used to create, store, or verify the smart contract, record, or signature…read more
